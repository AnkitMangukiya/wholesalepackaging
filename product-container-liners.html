Bulk Container Liners

Bulk container liners convert standard 20-foot and 40-foot ISO shipping containers into efficient bulk transport vessels for dry powders, granules, and liquid cargo. By eliminating intermediate packaging like drums, bags, and IBCs, container liners dramatically reduce cost per tonne and maximize container utilization for high-volume bulk shipments.

Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

Bulk container liners offer one of the most significant cost-reduction opportunities available to high-volume bulk shippers. Instead of packing product into hundreds of drums, bags, or IBC totes, a single container liner fills the entire container volume in one operation — slashing packaging costs, handling labour, and freight charges.

Our dry container liner systems are manufactured from heavy-duty polyethylene film and are available in top-fill, back-fill, and gravity configurations to match your loading facility's capabilities. The liner is installed in minutes and then filled directly by pneumatic or gravity loading systems.

Liquid bulk container liners — commonly called flexitanks — are engineered for liquid cargo including non-hazardous chemicals, food-grade liquids, wine, juice, and industrial liquids. Flexitank systems can carry up to 24,000 litres in a standard 20-foot container.

Wholesale Packaging supplies container liner systems to bulk shippers, logistics operators, food exporters, and chemical distributors across Canada.
