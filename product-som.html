SOM — Sewn Open Mouth Bags

SOM (Sewn Open Mouth) bags are multiwall kraft paper bags with a sewn-closed bottom and an open top for filling. One of the most economical and widely used industrial paper bag formats globally, SOM bags deliver reliable strength and filling line compatibility for cement, chemicals, animal feed, and a wide range of powdered or granular products.

Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

The SOM bag is one of the most enduring formats in industrial packaging — valued for its simplicity, strength, and low cost. The sewn bottom provides exceptional resistance to tearing and bursting under load, while the open top accepts a wide variety of filling methods including gravity, auger, and pneumatic systems.

After filling, the open top is typically closed by sewing, heat-sealing, or gluing depending on the specific application and downstream handling requirements. This flexibility makes SOM bags compatible with a vast range of production lines without specialist equipment.

Multiple kraft paper plies are available in 2-ply to 5-ply constructions. Inner plies of polyethylene or bitumen can be added for products requiring protection from moisture or improved resistance to aggressive chemical contents.
