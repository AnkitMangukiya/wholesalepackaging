Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

FIBC bag liners serve as a critical secondary barrier inside your bulk bags, significantly extending the protection they provide for sensitive materials. Polyethylene liners create a complete moisture and contamination barrier between your product and the woven outer bag.

We offer liners in a wide range of materials and thicknesses. Food-grade LDPE liners meet FDA and EU 10/2011 food contact regulations. Antistatic and conductive liner options are available for Type C and Type D FIBC applications. Multi-layer co-extruded barrier films provide enhanced oxygen, moisture, and aroma barriers for demanding storage conditions.

Liners are manufactured to custom dimensions that precisely match your FIBC size, ensuring a form-fitting installation that maximises filling efficiency. Closure options include heat seal, tie-string, zip, and welded ends — choose the right system for your process.

Wholesale Packaging supplies FIBC liners across Canada as standalone products or as part of a complete bulk bag solution.
