Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

The APC Peanut Tote is the North American agricultural industry's standardized container for peanut handling. Built to American Peanut Council specifications, these bags provide a consistent, hygienically sound solution that is universally compatible with peanut processing equipment across the industry.

Manufactured from 100% virgin polypropylene with food-safe components throughout, APC totes maintain peanut quality and cleanliness from harvest through to the shelling and processing facility. The standardized duffle top and spout bottom enables efficient clean filling in the field and controlled discharge into processing equipment.

Beyond food safety, these totes are engineered for the physical demands of agricultural logistics — including forklift handling, trailer transport, and outdoor storage. UV-stabilized fabric and reinforced loops ensure durability throughout the supply chain.

We supply APC peanut totes across Canada to agricultural operations, peanut processors, and food manufacturers who need a reliable, specification-compliant bulk container.
