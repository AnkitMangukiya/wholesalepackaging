Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

When handling edible goods, pharmaceutical ingredients, or nutraceuticals in bulk, product safety and regulatory compliance are non-negotiable. Food and pharmaceutical grade FIBCs are engineered using only virgin-grade polypropylene with no recycled content, harmful UV stabilizers, or unapproved additives.

All food grade bags are manufactured in dedicated clean facilities with controlled hygiene procedures. Standard food-safe PE inner liners provide a direct-contact barrier that keeps contents isolated from the outer woven fabric, preventing any possibility of fibre contamination.

Full regulatory documentation is provided with every order — including Certificates of Conformity, FDA compliance declarations, EU food contact statements, and product test reports. This paperwork is essential for HACCP audits, retailer compliance programmes, and export documentation.

Wholesale Packaging supplies food and pharma grade FIBCs throughout Canada for grain handlers, flour mills, sugar refiners, spice packers, animal feed producers, and pharmaceutical ingredient processors.
