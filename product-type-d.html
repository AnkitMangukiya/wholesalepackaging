Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

Type D FIBC bags represent the most advanced electrostatic safety solution in bulk bag technology. Manufactured from specially engineered dissipative polypropylene fabric, they safely neutralize surface static charges by dissipating energy at a controlled rate — without any requirement for earthing cables.

The key advantage over Type C bags is the elimination of operator dependency on grounding. With Type C bags, a forgotten ground connection can turn a safe bag into a potential ignition source. Type D bags remove this risk entirely — static is managed automatically by the fabric itself.

Type D bags are fully compatible with ATEX zone requirements and are independently tested and certified to IEC 61340-4-4. They are the recommended choice for operations handling explosive powders, fine chemicals, toners, and other materials in hazardous area classifications.
