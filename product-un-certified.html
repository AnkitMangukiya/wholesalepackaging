Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

When shipping regulated dangerous goods in paper bags, the packaging must carry a valid UN performance certification. UN Certified Industrial Paper Bags have undergone rigorous independent testing including free-fall drop tests, top-load stacking tests, and vibration tests in accordance with UN Model Regulations.

The UN certification mark on each bag identifies the bag type, material, performance standard, packing group approval, maximum gross mass, and the certifying body — providing instant verification of compliance for customs authorities, transport inspectors, and receiving facilities.

These bags comply with Transport Canada's Transportation of Dangerous Goods (TDG) regulations, US DOT 49 CFR, IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations, and IMDG Code — covering all major modes of transport used in Canadian and international commerce.
