PBOM — Pinch Bottom Open Mouth Bags

PBOM (Pinch Bottom Open Mouth) bags combine the structural stability of a pasted flat-bottom seal with the versatility of an open-top fill design. Widely used for pet food, animal feed, flour, and chemical powders, these bags stand upright on their pinch-sealed base for professional shelf presentation and efficient palletization.

Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

The PBOM bag design has become a preferred choice for bagged consumer and agricultural products that need to stand upright and present well at retail or trade distribution level. The pinch-sealed flat bottom creates a stable, self-standing base that remains flat when filled — delivering a professional appearance on pallets and shelves.

Multiple layers of high-quality kraft paper provide excellent puncture resistance, burst strength, and stacking performance. Optional inner plies of polyethylene or wax-coated paper are available for products requiring additional moisture protection.

The smooth outer kraft paper surface accepts high-resolution multicolour flexographic printing, making PBOM bags ideal for branded consumer products such as pet food, bird seed, potting mix, and specialty agricultural products where shelf appeal matters.
