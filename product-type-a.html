Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

Type A FIBC bulk bags are the industry's most popular and economical option for storing and moving dry, free-flowing materials. Constructed from conventional woven polypropylene, they offer no mechanism for static dissipation — making them perfectly suited for non-combustible products in low-risk environments.

Each bag is fitted with four reinforced lifting loops at the corners, allowing safe forklift handling throughout warehouse and logistics operations. Construction options include U-panel, circular, 4-panel, and baffle configurations to match your specific flow and stacking requirements.

At Wholesale Packaging, we supply Type A bags across Canada with competitive wholesale pricing. Whether you need standard off-the-shelf sizes or fully customized bags with printed branding, inner liners, and specialty closures — we can source and deliver to your exact specifications.

Safety Note: Type A bags should only be used with non-flammable materials in environments free from flammable solvents or combustible dust clouds. For hazardous environments consider Type B, C, or D bags.
