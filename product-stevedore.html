Product Overview

Everything You Need to Know

Stevedore FIBCs are a specialized class of flexible intermediate bulk containers designed specifically for port and marine environments. They feature extra-heavy woven polypropylene fabric and reinforced stevedore-style lifting loops that distribute crane and port equipment loads more evenly.

Woven polypropylene sandbags are among the most versatile products in emergency and construction management. Their tight woven structure provides excellent resistance to abrasion, UV degradation, and moisture — making them ideal for flood barriers, erosion control, retaining walls, and ballasting.

Both product lines are available in multiple sizes and configurations to match your specific operational requirements. Colour options are available for coding, identification, or regulatory compliance.

Wholesale Packaging supplies stevedore FIBCs and sandbags across Canada to port operators, construction companies, civil engineering firms, municipalities, and emergency management agencies.
